Key Takeaways & Opportunities

The markets in Portugal, Greece, Serbia, Croatia, Bulgaria, and South Africa present a landscape ripe with opportunity. The robust private label manufacturing infrastructure offers a low-barrier entry point for new brands and retailers. Success hinges on aligning with key consumer trends: the demand for high-quality, natural ingredients and the trust instilled by internationally recognized certifications. While direct C-level contact is challenging to source from public data, the identified companies represent strong potential partners for sourcing, distribution, and co-development.
